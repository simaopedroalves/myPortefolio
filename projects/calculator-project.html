HORTA D'RIA

Welcome to our garden. I created this website inspired by the Google form that Tiago, owner of Horta d'Ria, provides to his customers weekly.

I saw an opportunity in that form to create a small online store, so I decided to get to work.

I had a chat with Tiago, who gave me permission to work with his photos, and I started using my imagination.

I began by envisioning the structure of the main page and the respective product families. I looked to major applications and websites for inspiration to challenge myself and create an online store from scratch.

There were numerous challenges in this project, from creating the shopping cart, to structuring the product data, and processing data for delivery.

I grew as a programmer and realized that with resilience and persistence, results eventually come to light.

Today, the website is designed to help Horta d'Ria's customers place their orders more conveniently.
